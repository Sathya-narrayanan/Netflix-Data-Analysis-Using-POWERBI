# Netflix-Data-Analysis-Using-Power-BI

## 🔹 Project Overview

Analyzed Netflix dataset to understand content trends and user behavior

Built interactive dashboards to track genre performance, subscriptions, and customer demographics

Focused on content distribution, watch time, and subscription patterns

Delivered insights for content strategy and customer engagement

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies Used

SQL → Data extraction, transformation, and analysis

Microsoft Excel → Data cleaning and preprocessing

Power BI → Interactive dashboard creation and visualization

## 🔹 Key Features

Genre-wise content and watch time analysis

Subscription plan analysis across countries and demographics

Customer segmentation based on age, gender, and location

Show release trends and content growth analysis

Rating-based analysis of Netflix content

## 🔹 Data Analysis Performed

Analyzed genre distribution to identify viewer preferences

Studied year-wise content production and additions

Evaluated subscription plans across countries and user segments

Segmented users by age group, gender, and geography

Analyzed watch time across genres and demographics

## 🔹 Dashboard Insights

### Genre Analysis

Nearly equal distribution across genres (~9–10%)

Romance and Action slightly lead

### Content Trends

Strong growth from 2020 to 2021 (~32% increase)

Stable production (~4000 shows/year)

### Customer Analysis

Higher user base in 60+ age group

Lower engagement from younger audience (13–30)

### Subscription Insights

Balanced usage across Basic, Standard, Premium

Standard plan dominates in most countries

### Geographic Insights

France and India have highest customer counts

Even distribution across global markets

## 🔹 Key Business Insights

No dominant genre → diverse audience preferences

High dependency on older age group users

Subscription plans show no strong upgrade trend

Growth driven by content expansion rather than engagement optimization

Underutilization of younger audience segment

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

Promote top genres (Romance/Action) through featured content & campaigns

Increase engagement in younger users via targeted marketing and offers

Optimize subscription strategy with plan-specific incentives

Expand regional strategies for underperforming countries

Use AI-based recommendation systems for personalized viewing

Maintain consistent content production (~4000 shows/year)

## 🔹 Project Outcome

Delivered insights on content strategy and audience behavior

Identified opportunities for user growth and retention

Enabled data-driven decision-making for content and subscriptions

Built a scalable dashboard for performance monitoring and analysis
